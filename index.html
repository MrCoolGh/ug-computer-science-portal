Welcome to UG Computer Science Portal

This portal is designed for University of Ghana Information Technology Students enrolled in the Distance Education programme. Here you can access all essential information you need:

Programme Timetable: View detailed class schedules for your courses
Exam Venue Checker: Find your assigned examination venue and details
Learning Resources: Access course materials and study guides
Exam Timetable: Check your examination schedule and dates

⚠️ Important Notice

The Exam Venue Checker is exclusively available for students studying at the Accra Learning Centre and main campus. Students at other learning centres should contact their respective centre coordinators for venue information.
